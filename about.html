Prompts & Provenance will be presented at the La Biennale d'Architettura 2025.

Through critical examination of machine learning systems, we explore how synthetic representations of place misrepresent, oversimplify, and erase localities.

The project advocates for ethical tools that protect and celebrate the nuanced poetics of locality.

A project by design-research studio interMediations.

On view 10th of May - 23rd of November 2025 @ La Biennale d'Architettura 2025.
